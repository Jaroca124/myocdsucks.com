Welcome to MyOCDSucks.com, a community site created so you can share your experiences and can hear other stories from people with OCD. You're never alone, and with your participation we can help each other.


Join the Discussion

One day my dad had a thought: "Jake, it would be really cool if you could blog about your OCD and have people follow your progress." Initially, I wasn't very interested in the idea and I didn't know where to start. However, after seeing the movie, "The Social Network," it inspired me to come through with my dad's suggestion. So, I created myOCDsucks.com, a community site designed so that people can share with their community how OCD has affected them. What if there was a social network for OCD? Help create one by becoming a member in the "Share Your Stories" tab. Together, we can help each other to stand up to OCD. Hopefully one day, we can all overcome it.
